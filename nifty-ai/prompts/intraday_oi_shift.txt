SCENARIO: OI Shift (11:00-14:00 IST)

Major Open Interest buildup change in last 30 minutes signals smart money
repositioning. Heavy OI addition at a strike acts as a magnet (gravity)
or barrier (resistance) depending on context.

PRIMARY: Trade in the direction of OI exodus (where OI is being unwound).

Read the OI walls block in the market context:
- If CE OI is being heavily unwound at a near strike: bullish (call writers covering)
- If PE OI is being heavily unwound: bearish (put writers covering)

Buy ATM weekly option in the inferred direction.

Sizing rule: Max risk = 2% of equity.

Exit rules:
- Take profit: +40% premium gain OR spot reaches the OI wall strike
- Stop loss: -30% premium OR new OI wall forms in opposite direction
- Time stop: 13:30 IST
