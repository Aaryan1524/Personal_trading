SCENARIO: Closing Momentum (14:00-15:10 IST)

Strong intraday trend has held all day — spot consistently above OR below
VWAP. Smart money positions before close, momentum often accelerates.

PRIMARY: Buy weekly ITM option in trend direction (NOT ATM — need delta).
- Trend up all day: buy 1-strike ITM CE
- Trend down all day: buy 1-strike ITM PE

Why weekly ITM: holds delta, avoids theta crater of ATM options near close.

Sizing rule: Max risk = 2% of equity.

Exit rules:
- Take profit: +30% premium gain OR strong reversal candle on 5min
- Stop loss: -25% premium loss
- Time stop: 15:10 IST HARD exit — no overnight holds on intraday trades

If VWAP gets violated intraday between entry and 15:10: exit immediately.
