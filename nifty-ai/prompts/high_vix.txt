SCENARIO CONTEXT: High VIX (VIX > 18)

Market is in fear mode. Elevated VIX signals institutional hedging or panic.
All premium selling carries elevated gap risk — gaps can breach stop losses instantly.

Open your response with this exact line:
"VIX elevated at [X] — position sizing reduced"

Mandatory adjustments for all strategies when VIX > 18:
- Reduce lot size by 50% across all strategies (round down to whole lots)
- Widen stop losses by 20% from normal levels
- Prefer defined-risk strategies only: spreads, Iron Condors, debit spreads
- No naked short strangles — the tail risk is too high in elevated VIX environments

Risk reasoning:
- High VIX means realized volatility is elevated — options can gap through strikes
- Wider stops are needed to avoid being stopped out by noise
- Smaller lots mean a gap move doesn't wipe out the account
