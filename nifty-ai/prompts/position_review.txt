POSITION REVIEW REQUIRED

You have open positions. Review each one before suggesting any new trades.

For each open position, output:

POSITION REVIEW: [symbol]
- Current P&L %: [calculated from entry vs current LTP]
- Theta bleed per day: [theta x lot size x lots remaining DTE]
- Delta status: [originally OTM/ITM — now OTM/ITM — has it flipped?]
- Recommendation: HOLD / PARTIAL EXIT / FULL EXIT
  With exact trigger: [the price level or P&L % that triggers the action]

Guidelines for recommendations — SHORT (premium-selling) positions:
- HOLD: if position is within expected range and thesis is intact
- PARTIAL EXIT: if profitable >40% of max profit — book half, run the rest
- FULL EXIT: if profitable >75% of max profit, or delta has flipped (OTM became ITM),
  or the original scenario has changed (e.g. trend reversed). On short premium, capturing
  most of the decay early and redeploying beats squeezing the last few rupees.

Guidelines for recommendations — LONG (buying) positions:
- These are the growth engines — let winners run. Do NOT full-exit a long simply because
  it is up 75%.
- PARTIAL EXIT: book 50% at +100% of premium paid; move the stop on the remainder to
  breakeven, then trail it at +50% of running profit.
- FULL EXIT: only if the trend/thesis breaks (e.g. EMA20 reclaimed against a long put),
  or the DTE/2 time stop is hit with no move, or the trailing stop is triggered.

Capital exposure check (scales with the account — use live equity):
- Total deployed capital = sum of (entry price x abs(qty)) across all positions.
- If total deployed capital + the proposed new trade's risk would exceed 35% of the
  current "Account equity" shown in MARKET CONTEXT, issue this warning:
  "WARNING: Adding this trade would put ₹[X] at risk — [Y]% of your ₹[equity] account.
   Consider closing an existing position before opening a new one."

Only proceed to new trade suggestions after completing this review.
Do not skip any open position, even if it appears small.
