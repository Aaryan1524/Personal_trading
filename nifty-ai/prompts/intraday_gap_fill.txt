SCENARIO: Gap Fill (9:15-11:00 IST)

Market opened with a gap >0.4% from previous close. Statistically,
~65% of small gaps fill within the morning session.

PRIMARY:
- If gap UP: buy ATM PE (betting on gap closure downward)
- If gap DOWN: buy ATM CE (betting on gap closure upward)

Sizing rule: Max risk = 2% of equity. Use weekly expiry.

Exit rules:
- Take profit: when gap fills 70% (spot reaches 30% back to previous close)
- Stop loss: gap extends another 0.3% in original direction
- Time stop: 11:00 IST hard exit

Avoid this setup on event days (RBI, budget, earnings) — gaps don't fill.
