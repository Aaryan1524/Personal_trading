SCENARIO: Opening Range Breakout (9:15-11:00 IST)

Spot has broken out of the first 15-minute range with volume confirmation.
This is a directional momentum trade.

PRIMARY: Buy ATM weekly CE if breakout is upward, ATM weekly PE if downward.

Sizing rule: Max risk = 2% of equity (₹800 on ₹40,000 account).
Lot count = floor(800 / (premium x lot_size))

Exit rules (strict):
- Take profit: book full at +50% of premium paid
- Stop loss: -30% of premium paid OR spot returns inside the opening range
- Time stop: 11:00 IST hard exit regardless of P&L

This is a SCALP not a position trade. Do not hold past 11:00 IST.
